				User Commands

NAME
	python3 olympics.py

SYNOPSIS
	python3 olympics.py <-n> <noc>
	python3 olympics.py <-a>
	python3 olympics.py <-g> <event> <year>
	python3 olympics.py <-?>

DECSRIPTION
	This method prints out various information from an Olympics database according to user specifications. Combinations of flags are not supported.

OPTIONS

--atheltes -a <noc>
	  Prints a list of all of the athletes from a user-specified NOC. If the NOC search term is not in the database, nothing will be returned.

--goldmedals
	Prints all of the NOCs and how many gold medals they have won. NOCs with no gold medals are not printed.

--podiumfinish
	Prints all of the athletes who finished on the podium in a user-specified event and year.

--moreHelp
	Provides usage statements for all of the above functions.
